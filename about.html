Who I Am

Victory Nonye Ibeagi

Hello! I am Victory Nonye Ibeagi, a passionate and detail-oriented Web Developer based in Nigeria. I have a deep love for building websites that are not only visually appealing but also fast, responsive, and accessible on every device.

My journey in web development started with a curiosity about how websites work. That curiosity quickly turned into a passion as I learned HTML, CSS and JavaScript — the three core building blocks of the modern web. I enjoy every step of the process, from planning a layout to writing the last line of CSS.

My most recent project was a full replica of the FirstBank Nigeria Business Banking page — a complex, multi-section website with an auto-rotating hero slider, CSS-only dropdown menus, a floating login panel, and responsive layouts for different screen sizes. It challenged me and sharpened my skills significantly.
